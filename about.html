About Freshman Finance

Empowering the next generation to master their money

Our Story

Freshman Finance was born from a simple observation: most college students never received formal financial education before entering the real world. Credit cards, student loans, budgeting, and investing can feel overwhelming when you're navigating them for the first time.

We created this blog to bridge that gap. Through practical tips, real-world examples, and honest conversations about money, we help freshmen (and all college students) build financial literacy and make smart decisions about their future.

What We Believe

  • Financial literacy is a superpower. Understanding money gives you choices and freedom.
  • You're never too young to start. The habits you build now compound into wealth later.
  • Mistakes are learning opportunities. Everyone makes financial missteps—what matters is learning and growing.
  • Money shouldn't be taboo. We talk openly about salaries, debt, and savings without judgment.
  • Your situation is unique. There's no one-size-fits-all approach to personal finance.

Our Topics

We cover everything from the fundamentals to advanced strategies:

  • Budgeting and saving strategies
  • Understanding student loans and debt
  • Building credit and credit scores
  • Side hustles and income generation
  • Investing for beginners
  • Emergency funds and financial security
  • Credit cards and smart spending
  • Life after college and career finances
